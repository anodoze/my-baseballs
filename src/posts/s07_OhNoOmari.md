### Oh No Omari

Former Tree Frog Omari Bryant's pitching struggles are over for now -- the Crapo Player Removal Rock has moved him to Center Field to take a crack at batting. Just has he's finding his pace, it appears he's been afflicted by the Corruption. The Rock's manager has recklessly declined to quarantine Bryant, longtime fans look on nervously as his condition progresses. 

### Rebound Containment

Teams around the league are fighting the uphill battle to keep the Corruption contained. Some players spend many games trying to spread it. The Eugene Occult Sound Grounds' SP4 Kid Nova had half an inning. Corrupted by the Wheel Chair Accessible Mountains' Lickety Montgomery, two batters later they turned around and attempted a rebound corruption on Seraphina Foster and was immediately Contained, replaced by a pitcher pulled off the bench for the remainder of the game.
